Rio+23 WHS India Certification Program


Rio+23 WHS India Certification Program is a premiere and prestigious short-term course study for Indian students.

The program focusses on four key themes of WHS 2016:



a. Humanitarian effectiveness

b. Reducing risk and managing vulnerability

c.Transformation through innovation and

d. Serving the needs of people in conflict.

-Win Prestigious National Recognition in the National Hall of Fame (23 national level winners)

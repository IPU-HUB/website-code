PATENT CASE STUDY
“A country without a patent office and good patent laws is just a crab, and can’t travel any way but sideways and backways.” - Mark Twain Do patents foster innovation or hinder it? Well, this is not the debate here. If you think you know what it takes to get a patent, why not show you can get one? Answer questions, soak in loads of information and answer more questions, and face the jury. Who said getting a patent was easy! Are you up to the challenge?
1. The event will be conducted in three rounds.
2. The first round will consist of a quiz of duration 15mins.
3. It will test basic knowledge about intellectual property rights and patents. 10 participants will qualify for the next round.
4. The second round will be based on comprehension and be of duration 15mins.
5. Slides will be showed for 1min and 30s will be provided to answer the questions that follow based on it. Five participants will qualify for the round round.
6. The final round will require the particpants to convince the jury on why they should be granted a patent for a hypotheical scenario.
LINKS FOR PREPARATION OF FIRST ROUND
1. WIPO - About Intellectual Property - http://www.wipo.int/about-ip/en/
2. Indian Patents - FAQs -- http://www.indianpatents.org.in/faqpat.htm
2 PM, 8 April 2016

OVERVIEW

MASH project a non-profit youth led organisation is organising an 8 hour intensive workshop on the 'Internet of Things'. Internet Of Things, in layman terms, is the network of objects with embedded electronics to facilitate the exchange and collection of data. With more devices having WiFi capabilities now, probably LiFi capabilities in the future and having sensors built into them, the technology costs have, and will go down, thus, creating perfect headwinds for the Internet of Things.
We wish to disseminate relevant skills and provide hands-on experience. For further details read on.
Introduction about the ‘Internet of Things and Embedded Systems’
- Introduction to micro-controllers and Arduino
- Understanding I/O


These are some of topics that would be covered during the workshop.
We invite anyone who has an inclination towards coding, who loves robotics and sundry.
